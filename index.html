About the Event

Sparking Innovation

Hackothsav2k24 is a 36-hour national-level hackathon hosted by Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology and Management. This event invites tech enthusiasts from across the nation to collaborate, innovate, and create solutions for real-world problems. It's a platform to showcase your skills and make a tangible impact on technology's future.


Participants will have the opportunity to work on challenging projects, pushing the boundaries of their creativity and technical expertise. This hackathon is designed to foster innovation and teamwork, providing a unique environment for learning and growth.


With a prize pool of Rs. 1,00,000, Hackothsav2k24 rewards exceptional talent and hard work. Open to both seasoned programmers and beginners, the event promises learning, workshops, and networking opportunities with industry experts. Form your teams and start brainstorming now. Join us for an unforgettable innovation journey at Hackothsav2k24!
